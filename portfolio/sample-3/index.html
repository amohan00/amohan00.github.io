Written Reflection

Genre Remix

In my logo/wordmark assignment, I made various decisions concerning color, fonts, and other aspects of the visual mode. To begin with, I chose a font that is easy to read, because my company is a restaurant that caters to all people. I still wanted it to stand out, so I chose a font that had some style and uniqueness to it. This way, people can recognize the font and associate it with my business. Next, I chose to use warm colors such as red, orange, and yellow, because these colors are commonly associated with food. Studies also show that these colors incite a feeling of hunger in people. For example, popular restaurants such as Applebee’s. Chili’s, Ruby Tuesday, Wendy’s, McDonald’s, and others all take advantage of these colors, so I chose to as well.

If I were creating a logo or watermark for a company that was not a restaurant, I would focus more on the symbolism of my design rather than the fonts and colors. I could use a design that does not need words, and people should still be able to associate my logo with my company based on the symbolism alone.
