Pecha Kucha

I was very proud of all of the work I completed in this course, particularly the soundscape, movie genre remix, wordmark, and overall website design. But I would like to focus specifically on my Pecha Kucha presentation, which I was extremely satisfied with.

To begin with, I researched the design principle of constraint, which I was able to use successfully in various places of my website. In the actual construction of my presentation, I used a color scheme that was accessible to my users and matched the design of my website. I also made sure that my slides were not too text heavy, and I made good use of pictures to illustrate my points. If I could improve my work further, I would add even more images because the pictures I chose to use were so effective. Finally, I believe that the actual delivery of my presentation was well done, and I am proud of the way I was able to embed my slides on my website. Overall, I was very satisfied with my completion of the Pecha Kucha assignment.
