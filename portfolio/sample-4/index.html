Website Design

While constructing my website, I made many choices that were influenced by existing websites that I thought had extremely successful designs. For example, I was inspired by Wikipedia’s simplicity and extremely convenient usability. In addition, I was impressed with how clean and formal Apple’s website looks, so I attempted to replicate some of their features in my design.

To begin with, Apple’s navigation bar draws attention to itself and is clearly labelled, so I also designed a navigation bar that would improve the usability of my site for my users. Next, my website uses darker background colors and an attention-grabbing header, similar to Apple’s website. Finally, I chose to use a picture on my homepage to immediately engage my audience, just like Apple uses an image of their iPhone.

If I were to revise my design further based on this comparison with Apple’s website, I could add a larger variety of background colors to my homepage. Apple uses contrast between black and white to make their page even more intriguing and I believe that I could be even more successful by doing so as well.
