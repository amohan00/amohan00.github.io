Vocal Removal

The third Audacity tutorial assignment, which focused on vocal removal and isolation, proved to be extremely challenging for me. I tried completing the assignment using a variety of different songs and audio files and ended up spending hours trying to create the perfect audio clip. Eventually, I conducted my own outside research as well and tried to find tutorials and techniques to help me successfully remove the vocals from a song. Finally, I was able to remove the vocals to What a Wonderful World by Louis Armstrong to the best of my ability. If I listen closely, I can still hear some of Louis Armstrongâ€™s words in certain parts of the song, but I am still extremely proud of my effort and the final product.

By facing this obstacle, I learned that even though I was familiar with Audacity, I still had much to learn. There is always room to practice even if you are an expert at something. I also improved my skills considerably while conducting outside research to help me with this assignment. I was able to put them to good use in the next Audacity tutorial and my soundscape.
