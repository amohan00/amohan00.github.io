Some Notes

New Rules

Buy-ins are increased to $35, and we are increasing the money up for grabs each week to $20! (Details listed below).

We will play with two "conferences" this year, which will operate as two completely seperate leagues (2 drafts, player pools, standings)

The regular season will be from NFL weeks 1-14. The four best records from each conference will make the playoffs, NFL weeks 15-17 (details listed below).

When the championship and third-place games roll around, the competing teams are from different conferences and might have some overlapping players. The lineups will be set offline, so the competitors will not be able to see each other's lineups.
