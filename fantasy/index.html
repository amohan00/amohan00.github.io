Some Notes

New Rules

We will play with two "conferences" this year, which will operate as two completely seperate leagues (2 drafts, player pools, standings)

The regular season will be from NFL weeks 1-14. The four best records from each conference will make the playoffs. When the championship and third-place games roll around, the competing teams are from different conferences and might have some overlapping players. The lineups will be set offline, so the competitors will not be able to see each other's lineups.

We are adding an additional FLEX position to our rosters to help make each 8-person league as competitive and free agency as challenging as our usual 12-person leagues.
